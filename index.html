02 Three Concepts For Hungryroot

Three Ways In, At Three Scales

One flagship series, one single film, and one fast edit from footage you already have. We film and produce at every level, so where you start is up to you.

01
The Series · Flagship, ongoing
"Health Journey"
Back in 2021 Hungryroot made a short series called Health Journey that followed real customers changing how they eat. It ran four parts, picked up a few hundred views, and stopped. The instinct was right. What it needed was a real commitment behind it.

Health Journey comes back as a returning documentary series. Each episode follows one real person over a few months as they rebuild the way they cook and eat at home, with a Hungryroot chef in their corner the whole way. Chef Kara, Chef Jason, or Chef Danielle helps them plan, shop, cook, and actually stick with it.

You meet someone who is sick of takeout and has no idea where to start. You watch the first messy week in their kitchen, and you are there a few months later when making dinner stops feeling like work.

Queer Eye pulled in millions by putting one real person and one real problem at the center of every episode, with experts who genuinely help. Health Journey does that for the part of life that happens three times a day, and Hungryroot is the partner who makes the change stick.
Why long-form episodic content wins long-term
One video gets watched and forgotten. A returning series is what compounds, because the whole Legendeering idea is that trust is built in small moments repeated over time, and every episode is one more of them. A year of episodes leaves you with an audience that keeps choosing you, not a spot someone scrolled past once.
02
A Single Film · one-time
"The Homepage Film"
Right now the Hungryroot homepage runs entirely on animation and photography. For a brand with 14 million deliveries behind it, there is no video anywhere on the page.

This one is a single film, not a series. Two to three minutes at the top of the site: what Hungryroot is, who it is for, and what it actually feels like to eat this way, told through one or two real customers and Ben's own reason for starting it.

A visitor lands on the page, presses play, and in three minutes understands the whole thing better than the feature list ever gets across.

It is the one asset the site is missing, and the kind of film a strong landing page tends to be built around.
Why one brand film is worth making
Your homepage does the most important job on the site, and right now it does it with a feature list. Legendeering starts with story before facts, because facts on their own rarely move anyone. A single film gives a first-time visitor the feeling behind the product in less time than it takes to read the page.
03
From Footage You Already Have · fast
"New Cuts From Your Existing Library"
Hungryroot already has 211 videos on its channel, and one 31-second spot has done more than 40 million views. Most of that reach was paid, and the library keeps growing every week.

This one is not a shoot. It is an edit. We take footage Hungryroot already owns and cut fresh paid-ad creative from it: new hooks, new angles, new lengths built for Meta, YouTube, and TikTok. A short sales video for the site can come out of the same material.

Fresh creative is the biggest lever on what a paid account gives back. The footage exists and most of the cost is already spent, so this is the quickest way to put video against a number the team is already spending against.

It is also the easiest place to begin. Send us the drive, and a couple of weeks later you have a batch of new ads to test.
Why fresh cuts from your own footage win
The footage is already shot and most of the spend is already committed, so this is the fastest way to see what stronger video does to a real number. And even a paid ad works harder when it leads with a story instead of a sell, which is Legendeering applied to the creative you are already running.
03 Lead With Value

The Legendeering Framework

The methodology behind every Talex production, and behind the three concepts above. Five directives, one core principle.

Legendeering is Talex's proprietary strategy. The core principle is straightforward: lead with value, always. The brand earns trust by giving without expectation, and that trust grows over time because people keep choosing to watch.

Five directives drive it:

  • ·Give Without Expectation. Value delivered without strings. The kind of generosity that builds loyalty money cannot buy.
  • ·Be Relentlessly Authentic. Say true things. Show real work. Speak like a human being.
  • ·It's Not About You. It's About Them. The audience has to come first. It's all about their wants and needs and making something they will choose to watch.
  • ·Start With Story, Follow With Facts. Story gives information shape, context, and emotional weight. Facts alone rarely move people.
  • ·Consistency Is Key. Trust is built in moments, repeated over time.

Hungryroot is expert at getting the right food to the right person at scale. Talex would add the video: a documentary series, a single film for the homepage, and fresh cuts from the footage you already have, each one built on the method above. The three concepts came straight out of it.
